How Lady Qaleen Baf Works
We connect Afghan women weavers directly with international buyers. Through digital training and storytelling, we ensure every carpet carries a voice, a legacy, and a future.

Our Vision

Lady Qaleen Baf envisions an Afghanistan where women lead through art, innovation, and independence. Our goal is to help rural women earn income, revive ancient art, and build a sustainable future through technology and culture.
